About us

Welcome to CycloElite, your go-to spot for all things cycling! Established in 1999, we are a family-owned business offering top-quality bikes, accessories, and services. Our friendly staff is passionate about biking and dedicated to helping you find the perfect gear. Whether you're experienced or just starting, we provide a wide range of products from leading brands and expert maintenance services. Join us for rides, workshops, and events as we promote a healthy, active lifestyle and a sustainable future.
